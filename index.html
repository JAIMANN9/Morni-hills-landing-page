About Our Adventures

Discover the scenic beauty of Morni Hills through cycling

Morni Hills, located 45km from Chandigarh, stands at an elevation of 1,267 metres and offers some of the most breathtaking cycling experiences in Haryana. Our community is passionate about exploring the rugged terrain, scenic lake routes, and challenging mountain trails that make this region a cyclist's paradise.

Join our growing community of adventure enthusiasts as we explore the hidden gems of Morni Hills, from the serene waters of Tikkar Taal to the historic Morni Fort. Every ride is an opportunity to connect with nature and push your limits.
